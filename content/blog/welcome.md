I'm Jakub — a cybersecurity student who spends most of his free time taking
things apart to understand how they actually work. This blog is where I write
that process down.

## What you'll find here

- **CTF writeups** — step-by-step breakdowns of challenges I solve, from reverse
  engineering to network forensics. I try to explain not just *what* worked, but
  *why*.
- **Project notes** — the messy middle of building things like my Autonomous
  Security Operations Platform and ML-powered IDS.
- **Lessons from the lab** — networking experiments, tooling I'm learning, and
  the occasional dead end (those teach the most).

## Why write at all?

Two reasons. First, writing forces me to actually understand a thing instead of
just getting it to work. Second, the security writeups that helped me most when I
was starting out were written by people one step ahead of me. This is me paying
that forward.
